"Lagenda Hotel's Facilities"



1. SWIMMING POOL





This is the perfect place for a relaxing outdoor swim in Lagenda Hotel.
This rooftop pool is surrounded by palm trees and colourful lounge chairs for hotel guests to wind down.
After a luxurious swim at the pool, you can enjoy drinks at the poolside bar, Mai Bar, while taking in grand views of sunsets.



2. FITNESS CENTRE





Each machine was designed to complement the way the human body moves, offering athletes seamless, efficient workouts.
The entire line of equipment connects to the MyWellness app to track your progress and help you hit new personal bests at future hotel workouts.
In addition to a traditional gym, this sleek fitness facility also offers a rock climbing wall and fun workout classes.
